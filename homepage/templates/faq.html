    Frequently Asked Questions

    How do I purchase an instrument?

    Simply create an account online, and then from there you can browse our catalog where we have many instruments at a great price. Then from there, you can have us ship the instrument to you, or you can come pick it up in the store.

    What is the warranty on your instruments?

    We want to make sure you are covered when you get a new instrument, so the standard warranty is 2 years, and you can purchase an additional 2 years at the time of purchase.

    Are you able to fix instruments

    We sure can! We have a full repair shop in our store, and we accept shipments to be able to get those repairs done. So if you live far away, we are happy to make those repairs!
